How We Work

We follow a structured and efficient workflow to deliver high-quality renewable energy solutions with precision and reliability.

Step 01

Site Inspection & Feasibility Check

We begin with a detailed site inspection to evaluate wind turbine conditions, solar potential, infrastructure readiness, and overall feasibility for energy optimization.

Step 02

Engineering Plan & Strategy

Our experts prepare a detailed engineering roadmap, including safety checks, component planning, logistics scheduling, and maintenance or installation strategy tailored to your site.

Step 03

Execution & Technical Operations

We carry out turbine repairs, solar installations, component replacements, electrical work, and all required field engineering with international-grade safety standards.

Step 04

Testing, Launch & Ongoing Support

After completion, we conduct full system testing and performance checks. Our team continues to provide monitoring, maintenance, and long-term technical support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our wind turbine services, solar installations, repairs, and renewable energy solutions.

1 What services does Sri VBS Wind Technologies provide?

We provide end-to-end renewable energy services including wind turbine maintenance, solar power system installation, component repair & supply, site infrastructure support, logistics, and engineering consulting.

2 Do you offer both wind and solar energy solutions?

Yes, our company specializes in both wind turbine services and solar power engineering. We support industrial, commercial, and residential renewable energy projects with complete technical expertise and certified professionals.

3 How experienced is your technical team?

Our engineers and field technicians have over 15+ years of combined experience in turbine servicing, electrical systems, offshore/onshore operations, and solar power plant deployment.

We follow international safety protocols and industry best practices to ensure high-quality work.

1 How do you determine the cost of wind turbine maintenance?

The pricing depends on turbine height, type, component condition, location, and the type of service required‚Äîsuch as troubleshooting, component replacement, or full maintenance.

2 Do you charge separately for site inspection?

In most cases, basic inspections are offered at a nominal charge, but detailed technical analysis or multi-day inspections may have additional costs. This will be communicated clearly before service.

3 Is solar installation pricing fixed or customizable?

We offer fully customizable pricing based on capacity (kW/MW), panel type, inverter selection, wiring requirements, roof structure, and installation complexity.

1 Do you offer emergency breakdown support for wind turbines?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency service for turbine breakdowns, electrical failures, component issues, and sudden performance drops. Our rapid-response team ensures minimal downtime.

2 What kind of post-service support do you provide?

We offer AMC contracts, routine inspections, performance monitoring, component supply support, and technical assistance for both wind and solar systems.

3 How can I reach your support team quickly?

You can contact us through WhatsApp, direct call, or email. Our dedicated support team responds quickly to ensure smooth operation of your renewable energy system.
