About Us

Sri VBS Wind Technologies is a leading renewable energy service provider specializing in wind turbine maintenance, solar power installations, industrial electrification, and complete green energy solutions. With years of industry expertise, our mission is to deliver highly reliable, efficient and cost-effective power solutions that support a cleaner and sustainable future.

Our team consists of certified engineers, skilled technicians, and experienced field specialists dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of safety and performance. From large-scale wind farms to commercial solar projects, we provide end-to-end services including installation, inspection, preventive maintenance, fault repair, electrical testing, and 24/7 service support.

At Sri VBS Wind Technologies, we believe in strong client partnerships, transparent communication, and delivering results that exceed expectations. Our solutions are designed to improve power output, extend equipment lifespan, and ensure long-term operational efficiency for our clients across Tamil Nadu and beyond.
